When to build custom software vs use existing SaaS

Deciding between bespoke build and off-the-shelf tools? We break down the trade-offs so you can choose with confidence.

Not every problem needs custom software, but some do. Here’s how we help clients decide.

Use SaaS when: The problem is well understood, there are mature products, and your differentiators aren’t in the tool itself. You’ll move faster and cheaper.

Build custom when: Your workflow is unique, you’re competing on product, or you need deep integration with your own systems. Off-the-shelf will hold you back.

Hybrid is common. Many clients use SaaS for marketing, CRM, and ops, and custom software for the core product or internal tools. We help you draw the line.
