Process

How we take you from idea to live software.

Four clear stages from first call to production. Fixed scope at every step, so you always know what ships, when it ships, and what it costs.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We start with a short call to understand what you are building, who uses it, and what “done” looks like. Before design or code, we capture the product properly.

    • User roles, permissions and core journeys
    • Screens, data model and integrations for v1
    • Success criteria we can test against at handover
  2. 02

    Scope and quote

    You receive a fixed quote for the SaaS build or Enterprise scope: written features, delivery window, and payment terms. No open-ended hourly billing.

    • Tier, price and timeline agreed in writing
    • Feature list with acceptance criteria
    • 50/50 on SaaS builds; Enterprise milestones on the scoping call
  3. 03

    Build

    We build in focused sprints with a private live link early on. Each week you see real software: auth, core screens, integrations and polish, not slide decks.

    • Weekly demo and plain-language progress updates
    • Staging environment you can click through anytime
    • Scope locked; new ideas wait for a follow-on phase
  4. 04

    Handover

    You receive everything needed to run and extend the product without us: repository, hosting access, environment notes and a recorded walkthrough.

    • Full source code and deployment access
    • Handover documentation your team can follow
    • No licence fee, no dependency on our platform

Frequently asked questions

What does custom software cost?

SaaS builds start from £5,000 with fixed scope, written deliverables and 50% upfront / 50% on handover. Enterprise starts from £18,000, with milestones agreed on the scoping call.

How long does a software MVP take?

Focused SaaS builds can ship from 14 days; larger products and Enterprise scopes take longer depending on integrations, compliance and product surface. Recent projects include a live SaaS in 21 days, a forex research platform in 12 days, and a lead magnet tool in 9 days.

Does the client own the code?

Yes. You receive the full codebase, deployment access, handover documentation and everything needed to run the product yourself. There is no licence lock-in or platform dependency.

What should we build first?

Start with the product step that proves value fastest: an MVP workflow, product dashboard, customer portal, pricing workflow, or admin dashboard. On a discovery call we help you pick the smallest useful v1.

Custom build vs off-the-shelf SaaS?

Use off-the-shelf SaaS when the job is standard. Build custom when your users, data model, approvals or integrations need something owned.
