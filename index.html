FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you work internationally?

Yes. We’re based in the UK (Hampshire) and deliver remotely for clients across the UK and internationally. Most of our work is remote-first, so we work with founders and businesses wherever they’re based.

What’s the typical timeline for an MVP or custom project?

MVPs and custom projects often ship in 2–6 weeks depending on scope. We agree a fixed scope and timeline at the start so you know what to expect and when.

How do you charge - fixed price or hourly?

We prefer fixed scope and fixed price where possible, so you don’t get surprise invoices. We’ll outline what’s in scope and the cost before we start.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes. We work with early-stage founders, scaling startups, and established businesses. If you have a clear idea and are ready to build, we can help.
