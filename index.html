Frequently asked questions

What does custom software cost for an agency?

Standen scopes a first build from £5,000 with fixed scope and timeline. You pay 50% upfront and 50% on handover. The price covers workflow mapping, design, build, deployment and full code ownership.

How long does an agency software MVP take?

A typical first build ships in 14 days, one workflow, one system, clear handover. Recent projects include a live SaaS in 21 days, a forex research platform in 12 days, and a lead magnet tool in 9 days.

Does the client own the code?

Yes. You receive the full codebase, deployment access, handover documentation and workflow logic. There is no licence lock-in or platform dependency.

What should an agency build first?

Start with the workflow that costs the most time or margin: proposals, client reporting, delivery portals, or internal CRM. We map the bottleneck on a discovery call and scope the smallest useful version.

Custom build vs off-the-shelf SaaS?

Use SaaS when the workflow is standard. Build custom when your delivery model, data or approvals are unique. See our agency build vs buy guide.
