Founder-led builds: why it matters for SaaS founders

When the person scoping is three handoffs away from the person shipping, details die and timelines slip.

Questions to ask any build partner

  • Who attends the workflow mapping call?
  • Who writes the scope you sign?
  • Who builds, same person or a bench you have not met?
  • Who handles the first bug report after launch?

Why it matters for SaaS teams

Your clients hire you for direct senior attention. Your software vendor should work the same way. Founder-led delivery is not vanity, it is risk control on a fixed budget.

Use this as a working checklist inside your team first. When the same steps repeat every week and spreadsheets start breaking, that is usually the moment to scope one workflow, one user group, one measurable outcome and a fixed handover plan as an owned system.
