The SaaS kickoff checklist that stops projects drifting

Bad kickoffs create bad projects. Use the same checklist every time.

48 hours before kickoff

  • Brief signed and stored.
  • Access confirmed (not "requested").
  • Stakeholders on calendar with decision authority.
  • Success metric restated in client words.
  • Internal owner assigned in delivery queue.

During kickoff

  1. Confirm stages and communication channel.
  2. Show where status will live (portal or shared view).
  3. Agree response SLAs both directions.
  4. Book next checkpoint before hanging up.

Use this as a working checklist inside your team first. When the same steps repeat every week and spreadsheets start breaking, that is usually the moment to scope a secure portal for onboarding status, account actions, files, approvals and customer messages as an owned system.
