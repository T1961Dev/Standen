Replace status meetings with async client updates

Status meetings exist because nobody trusts the system. Replace meetings with a written rhythm.

Weekly async format

  • Done, shipped items with links.
  • Next, top three priorities.
  • Blocked, needs client or internal decision.
  • Risks, optional, only if real.

When to keep a live call

Negotiation, relationship repair, or genuine ambiguity, not readouts.
