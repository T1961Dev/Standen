Custom dashboard vs spreadsheets for SaaS ops

Spreadsheets work until product complexity makes them the risk: wrong version, no audit trail, no single view for founders.

Signs you have outgrown Sheets

Multiple tabs nobody trusts. No owner on rows. Founders cannot see blockers without a meeting.

What a custom dashboard gives you

One queue, clear owners, status your team updates daily, and reporting that does not require export gymnastics.

Cost and timeline

A focused dashboard v1 fits the Launch tier from £5,000 and typically ships in 14 days. Scale or Enterprise applies when the product needs more roles, integrations or compliance-oriented architecture.

Frequently asked questions

Will my team actually use it?

We design around existing habits, fewer fields, faster updates than maintaining a sheet.

Can we migrate existing data?

Yes. CSV import and one-time migration scripts are common in scope.
