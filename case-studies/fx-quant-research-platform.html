React, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, real-time data pipelines, REST & WebSocket APIs.

Full platform delivered in 12 days. Client feedback: "Standen delivered our entire research platform in 12 days - faster than anyone else could even start. The quality was exceptional and they hit every deadline." - Quantitative Research Lead.
