The challenge

The client needed a unified research environment for forex analysis. Market data, backtesting, and predictive signals were scattered, making it difficult to move from raw information to confident decisions.

We built a full-stack platform with market data views, automated backtesting, predictive analytics, and clean dashboard interfaces. The system made complex financial datasets easier to inspect, compare, and act on.
