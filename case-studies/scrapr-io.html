The challenge

Sales teams need qualified lead data quickly, but manual prospecting is slow and generic databases are often stale or incomplete.

What we delivered

Scrapr.io packages lead scraping, enrichment, storage, and export workflows into a fast SaaS dashboard. It gives users real email addresses and phone numbers for decision-makers without forcing them through manual research.
